Lesson 4: Lighting Types

Light Types in Three.js:

  • Ambient: Uniform lighting everywhere
  • Directional: Sun-like parallel rays
  • Point: Light bulb, radiates in all directions
  • Spot: Flashlight cone
  • Hemisphere: Sky/ground gradient
  • RectArea: Window or screen light
