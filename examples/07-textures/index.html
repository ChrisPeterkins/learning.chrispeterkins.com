Lesson 7: Textures & Maps

Texture Types:

  • Color Map: Base color/diffuse
  • Normal Map: Surface detail illusion
  • Roughness Map: Surface smoothness
  • Metalness Map: Metallic properties
  • AO Map: Ambient occlusion
  • Displacement: Actual geometry deformation

Textures bring materials to life in games!
